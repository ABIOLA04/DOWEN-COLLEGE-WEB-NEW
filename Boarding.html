Boarding Life

Welcome to Boarding Life

Our boarding facilities are a true "home away from home". Students are well taken care of by our dedicated hostel parents who ensure a safe, comfortable, and nurturing environment around the clock.

We believe that structured communal living builds character, resilience, and the interpersonal skills our students will carry throughout life. Every aspect of boarding at Dowen is designed with your child's wellbeing at its centre.

Requirements

Boarding Requirements

To ensure a smooth transition into boarding life, please have the following ready before your child's arrival on campus.

  1. 1 Completed admission form and signed boarding agreement
  2. 2 Medical report and up-to-date immunisation records
  3. 3 Personal belongings as listed in the school handbook
  4. 4 Parental consent form and emergency contact details
  5. 5 Payment of current boarding fees

Need the full handbook? Contact our admissions office for the complete list of boarding requirements and packing guidelines.

Worship & Spiritual Life
