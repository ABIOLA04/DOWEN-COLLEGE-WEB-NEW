Our Curriculum

A world-class dual curriculum combining the best of Nigerian and British education systems

Dual Curriculum

At Dowen College Lagos, we run both the Nigerian and British curricula, culminating in BECE (NECO), Cambridge Checkpoint in Year 9, and WASSCE & IGCSE in Year 12.

Expert Faculty

Highly qualified and experienced teachers guide students with well-equipped laboratories and teaching aids to make learning engaging across all subjects.

Exam Preparation

Students are prepared for BECE, Cambridge Checkpoint, WASSCE, IGCSE, SAT, TOEFL, and IELTS with meticulously supervised tutorials and homework.

Important: Students are not allowed to sit for any external examinations except those the school officially registers them for.

Homework diaries must be brought to school daily and signed by parents or guardians.

Full Tuition Scholarship

Students qualify for a full tuition scholarship for the next session by maintaining an average of 90% and above consistently for three terms in either Junior or Senior School.
